Privacy Policy

What We Collect

TalentSift collects account details, uploaded resumes, job descriptions, analysis history, payment status, usage events, and support messages needed to provide the service.

How We Use Data

We use data to authenticate users, analyze resumes against job descriptions, enforce plan limits, improve product reliability, process payments, and provide support.

Uploads And AI Processing

Resume files may be stored with our cloud storage provider and processed by AI providers to generate ATS scoring, skill matches, summaries, and recommendations.

Payments

Payments are handled by Razorpay. TalentSift stores payment references, plan status, and verification metadata, but does not store card numbers.
